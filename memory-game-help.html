Memory Game

The objective of the memory card game is to match all pairs of cards in the deck. There are a total of 8 pairs that you need to match - that's 16 cards in total!

To beat the game, find all the card matches before the timer runs out!

To find matches, turn over each card by clicking the card with your mouse, or tapping it with your finger if on a touchscreen device.

Note, you can only turn over one card at a time, and if a match isn't found after the second card, both cards will turn back over to its back-face.

Easy: You get 60 seconds to find all card matches!

Medium: You get 40 seconds to find all card matches!

Hard: You get 25 seconds to find all card matches!

Good luck!
