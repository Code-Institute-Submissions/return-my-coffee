Memory Game

The aim of the memory card game is to match all card pairs shown on screen. There are 8 pairs that you need to match - thatâ€™s 16 cards in total!

To win, find all card matches before the timer runs out!

To turn over a card, left-click the card with your mouse, or tap it with your finger when on a touchscreen device.

You can only turn over one card at a time. When two cards match, both cards will remain flipped.

Easy: You get 60 seconds to find all card matches!

Medium: You get 40 seconds to find all card matches!

Hard: You get 25 seconds to find all card matches!

Good luck!
