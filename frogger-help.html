Frogger

The objective of Frogger is to help the little frogs cross the road and reach the other side of the water. Be sure to avoid cars and don't let the frogs jump in the water as, unfortunately, these froggers can't swim.

Frogger is a bonus game of sorts. There is no target score that you need to reach to win, just see how many frogs you can help before the timer runs out!

To move up :
press key or key

To move down :
press key or key

To move left :
press key or key

To move right :
press key or key

Easy: You get 60 seconds!

Medium: You get 40 seconds!

Hard: You get 25 seconds!

Good luck!
