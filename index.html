Why hello there! I want to be the first to welcome to you this game.

Return myCoffee

While you are here, you have a task do to. It’s all very terrible, but I must tell you that someone has stolen your cup of coffee. Whom you say? I’m afraid I don’t know. But what I know is that the only way you can hope to get it back is by completing each challenge within this game. If you’re lucky, you might have your coffee in your grasp once again!

Now then, you must want to get to it - there’s no time to waste!

Are you curious about the game’s creator? Well, I welcome curiosity! Look below to read more about it.

This project started as a way for me to test and practice my newly gained JavaScript skills. I wanted to see what I could do, and what challenges I could make for myself. After completing one game, I did another, then another. Then the idea came to combine these games I had created together. To do that successfully, however, I needed something to tie it all together - which is how Return myCoffee took shape.
