Whack-A-Mole

The aim of whack-a-mole is to hit moles as they pop up from the holes. They move fast so keep your eye on the screen!

To win, reach score 25 (25 moles) before the timer runs out!

To whack a mole, left-click it with your mouse, or tap it with your finger on a touchscreen device. You'll only have a short time frame, so be quick!

The mole will look angry when whacked. This is how you know if a hit was successful!

Easy: You get 60 seconds to reach 25!

Medium: You get 40 seconds to reach 25 and the mole speed is quicker!

Hard: You get 25 seconds to reach 25 and the mole speed is even quicker than the last!

Good luck!
